Friday STEAM

Does your child have Fridays off of school? Enroll them in our Friday STEAM Camps! The camps will have a different STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Math) focus each week. As an added bonus, students will have time to work on homework and receive tutoring help from staff! Save up to 10% by enrolling your child for ALL Friday STEAM Camps this spring!

Fall Friday STEAM Camp Dates are Jan 10, 17, 24, 31, Feb 7, 14, 21, 28 ,March 6, 27, April 3, 10, 17, 24, May 1, 8, 15

Spring Semester Camps

January 20: Snow Science

Location: EUREKA!
Time: 8:30AM-5:30AM
Members: $30
Non-Member: $36

Does the cold never bother you anyway? Channel your inner Elsa and learn all about the science of that amazing white stuff that falls from the sky! Students will learn how to make “snow” at home, what causes snow, and so much more! Zip up your coat and slide on some googles as you’ll learn the science in snow.

February 17: Raging Roller Coaster

Location: Eureka!
Time: 8:30AM-5:30AM
Members: $30
Non-Member: $36

Did you know most roller coasters do not have an engine? They move using physics! Get ready for a wild, fun day where EUREKA! will immerse you in all the secrets to the physics of amusement parks! Learn the engineering and physics behind your favorite amusement park ride so next time you are there you can wow your friends with all you know!

March 12-13: Animal Planet

Location: Eureka!
Time: 8:30AM-5:30AM
Members: $60
Non-Member: $72

Calling all young zoologists! From barnyard animals to household pets, there are a lot of animals to explore! Join the EUREKA! staff as we dive into the Animal Planet to discover the world of zoology - how animals adapt to their environment and about the scientists who study and care for them. Students will even get the chance to learn about some of the animals we have right here at EUREKA!

March 16-20: The Body Inside

Location: Eureka!
Time: 8:30AM-5:30AM
Members: $155
Non-Member: $185

What exactly is a heartbeat? What propels our bodies when we jump? Are there really butterflies in your stomach when you get nervous? Exercise your brain and body at EUREKA! In this camp you will learn the anatomy of your own body and find out the mechanisms behind motions you perform.

May 21-22

Location: Eureka!
Time: 8:30AM-5:30AM
Members: $60
Non-Member: $72

Have you ever looked at a toilet paper tube, pipe cleaners, and an old newspaper and thought, I can make some cool art with this! If so this camp is for you! You will summon your inner engineer and artist, spending the camp designing and decorating cool art. In this camp one man’s trash will be repurposed into your own treasure!
